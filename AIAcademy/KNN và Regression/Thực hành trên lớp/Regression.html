Cơ sở hồi quy tuyến tính¶

Hồi quy tuyến tính đơn giản ( 1 biến độc lập)¶

$$y=\beta_0+\beta_1 x$$

  • $y$: bin ph thuc

  • $x$: bin c lp

  • $\beta_0,\beta_1$: tham s ca m hnh

Hồi quy tuyến tính nhiều chiều (nhiều biến độc lập)¶

Hi quy 2 bin: $y=\beta_0+\beta_1 x_2+\beta_2 x_2$

Hi quy $m$ bin: $y=\beta_0+\beta_1x_1+\beta_2x_2+\dots+\beta_mx_m$

  • $y$: bin ph thuc

  • $x$: bin c lp

  • $\beta_0,\beta_1,\beta_2,\dots,\beta_m$: tham s ca m hnh

Xác định giá trị của tham số mô hình¶

  • Xc nh sai s ( hm tn tht) ca m hnh.

  • Chn tham s sai s nh nht.

Mô hình lý thuyết¶

Gi s rng bin ph thuc $Y$ (output, dependent, response) c quan h tuyn tnh vi cc bin u vo (independent, predictor) $X_1,X_2,\dots,X_m$ bi cng thc

$$ Y=\beta_0+\sum\limits_{j=1}^m \beta_j X_j+\varepsilon $$

trong $\varepsilon\sim N(0,\sigma^2)$ bin sai s khng quan st c (error component)

Mục tiêu¶

c lng cc tham s $\beta_j$, phng sai $\sigma^2$, v s nh hng cc bin u vo i vi $Y$.

Gi s ta c cc $n$ quan st $$(x_{i1},\dots,x_{im},y_i), i=1,2,\dots,n $$

$$y_i=\beta_0+\sum\limits_{j=1}^m \beta_j x_{ij}+e_i, i=1,2,\dots,n $$

vi cc $e_i$ l cc sai s v cng phn phi vi $\varepsilon$

Ta s dng phng php bnh phng ti thiu c lng cc $\beta_j$ sao cho sai s nh nht $$SSE=\sum\limits_{i=1}^ne_i^2=\sum\limits_{i=1}^n (y_i-\beta_0-\sum\limits_{j=1}^m \beta_j x_{ij})^2 $$ $$\hat{\beta} =\arg\min SSE(\beta)$$

Tng bnh phng cc sai s (SSE): $$SSE=\sum\limits_{i=1}^n \hat{e}_i^2=ESS(\hat{\beta}) $$ H s $R^2$, $$ R^2=1-\dfrac{SSE}{SST}=1-\dfrac{\sum\limits_{i=1}^n (y_i-\hat{y}_i)^2}{\sum\limits_{i=1}^n (y_i-\bar{y})^2}$$

Kiểm tra độ chính xác của mô hình¶

  • Thng thng ta chia d liu thnh hai tp: tp hun luyn v tp kim tra. Khi xy dng m hnh ta khng c ly tp kim tra s dng.
  • Trong tp hun luyn ta trch mt phn d liu hun luyn gi l tp validation. M hnh c kim tra thng quan tp validation trn.
  • Vn chn kch thc tp validation?

Phương pháp cross-validation¶

  • Chia tp hun luyn thnh $k$ tp con (cng kch thc), ri nhau.
  • Mi ln kim tra th, hun luyn m hnh vi $k-1$ tp v dng tp cn li l tp validation.
  • M hnh cui cng c la chn da trn sai s hun luyn v sai s ca tp validation.

Hiệu chỉnh mô hình hồi quy (Regularized regression)¶

  • Hi quy tuyn tnh cc tiu ha hm tn tht (loss function)

  • Nu chn tt c cc bin c lp

  • Cc bin c lp ln nn cc h s ln dn n overfitting

  • Hiu chnh: a thm phn hiu chnh cc h s.

Ridge Regression¶

  • Hm tn tht $$L(\beta)=\sum\limits_{i=1}^n (y_i-\beta_0-\sum\limits_{j=1}^m \beta_j x_{ij})^2+\alpha\sum\limits_{j=0}^m \beta_j^2 $$

  • $\alpha$: tham s (cn c xc nh)

  • Nu $\alpha=0$: Ta c hi quy thng thng

  • Nu $\alpha$ ln: C th dn ti underfitting

def remove_missing_data(pX_train, feature_to_impute):
    X_train =  np.copy(pX_train)
    for i in range(X_train.shape[0]-1, 0, -1):
        for j in range(0, X_train.shape[1], 1):
            if feature_to_impute[j] != 0 and X_train[i, j] == 0:
                X_train = np.delete(X_train, i, 0)
                break
    return X_train

def mean_imputation_pure(pX_train, feature_to_impute):
	X_train =  np.copy(pX_train)
	for i in range(0, len(feature_to_impute)):
		if feature_to_impute[i] == 0:
			continue
		non_zeros = 0
		for j in range(0, X_train.shape[0]):
			if X_train[j, i] != 0:
				non_zeros += 1
		mean = np.sum(X_train[:, i])/float(non_zeros)
		for j in range(0, X_train.shape[0]):
			if X_train[j, i] == 0:
				X_train[j, i] = mean
	return X_train
